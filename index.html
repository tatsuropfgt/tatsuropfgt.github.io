Tatsuro Inaba (稲葉 達郎)

Tatsuro Inaba is a first-year Ph.D. student in the NLP Department at MBZUAI , supervised by Prof. Kentaro Inui and Prof. Gus Xia.
His research interests include training dynamics and hierarchical capabilities of both language and music models.
