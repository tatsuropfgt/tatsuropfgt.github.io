Tatsuro Inaba is a first-year Ph.D. student at the Tohoku NLP Group, Tohoku University, under the supervision of Prof. [Kentaro Inui](https://kentaro-inui.github.io/).
He previously completed his master’s studies at the [Speech and Audio Processing Lab](http://sap.ist.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp/EN/) at Kyoto University, where he was advised by Prof. [Kazuyoshi Yoshii](http://sap.ist.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp/members/yoshii/) and Prof. [Eita Nakamura](https://eita-nakamura.github.io/).
During his undergraduate studies, he was a member of the [Language Media Processing Lab](https://nlp.ist.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp/EN/) at Kyoto University, working under the guidance of Prof. [Sadao Kurohashi](https://www.nii.ac.jp/en/faculty/director/).

My research interests lie in the following areas:
- Music Information Retrieval (MIR)
  - mechanistic interpretability of music models
  - domain-specific model architecture for music [[APSIPA'24](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10849230/)]
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  - mechanistic interpretability of language models [[NAACL'25](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.15754)]
  - practical application of LLMs [[ACL'23](https://aclanthology.org/2023.acl-short.130/)]
